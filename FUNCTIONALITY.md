# Journey Planner MCP Server

This service enables AI assistants to help you plan journeys using public transport in Norway and the Nordic region.

## What Can It Do?

### Plan Your Trip

Get complete travel suggestions from one place to another using trains, buses, trams, ferries, and metro. Simply tell the AI where you want to go, and it will find the best routes for you.

The service provides:
- Multiple route options to choose from
- Step-by-step directions for each leg of the journey
- Walking directions to and from stops
- Expected crowding levels on vehicles
- Service alerts about delays or disruptions

### Check Departures

Find out when the next bus, train, or tram leaves from any stop or station. Get real-time information including:
- Scheduled and actual departure times
- Platform or stop information
- Delays and cancellations
- Which lines are running and where they're heading

### Find Locations

Search for addresses, place names, or points of interest and get their exact locations. Useful when you're not sure of the exact name or address of where you want to go.

## Typical Use Cases

**Daily Commute**
> "When is the next train from Oslo S to Nationaltheatret?"

**Planning a Day Trip**
> "How do I get from Bergen city center to Fløibanen?"

**Catching a Flight**
> "Plan a trip from Trondheim central station to Værnes airport, arriving by 14:00"

**Exploring Public Transport Options**
> "Show me all departures from Jernbanetorget in the next hour"

**Finding Your Way in an Unfamiliar City**
> "How do I get from my hotel at Storgata 10 to the Viking Ship Museum?"

**Checking for Disruptions**
> "Are there any service alerts affecting trains from Drammen today?"

## Coverage

The service covers public transport throughout Norway, including:
- National and regional trains
- Local and express buses
- City trams and metro systems
- Ferries and boats
- Airport express services
